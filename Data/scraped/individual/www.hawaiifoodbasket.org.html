COVID 19 PANDEMIC RESPONSE

As many of you know, The Food Basket has been closely monitoring the progression of the coronavirus (COVID19) and we are carefully following the recommendations to protect public health and help slow the spread of the virus in our communities. The health, well-being and safety of our communities is our highest priority.  We are working closely with government and community partners on an island-wide emergency food distribution plan in response to COVID 19 that will be posted shortly.

Due to the COVID 19 response, the following pantries/agencies have closed food distribution until further notice:

We are actively working to protect vulnerable community members. This work includes adjustments in the distribution of food to reduce contact or large gatherings of individuals with increasing drive-through distributions and scheduled appointments for food assistance. These solutions involve distribution of boxes with a multi-day supply of shelf-stable food and locally procured fresh produce for individuals & families. We are distributing through islandwide `Ohana Drops and home delivery for those with limited transportation or compromised health.

Mahalo for your kokua to help halt the spread of this pandemic.

If you or someone you know, is experiencing food insecurity, please call 933-6030 or use the contact form on this site to send us a message.

 

 

Programs

Through a mix of supplemental food programs, traditional food pantries and congregate meal sites, we aim to provide people with access to the type of food assistance they need. In addition to food, these community food access programs provide social contact and promote community cohesiveness critical components in building individual and Hawai`i Island resiliency.
