  • COVID-19 (Coronavirus) Emergency Fund Campaign
    The regularly scheduled monthly food distributions at our 11 pantry sites in Greene are continuing as planned, with safety protocols in place, following instructions from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.

    We have no plans to cease food distribution at this time, as we feel that our services are needed now more than ever, and are transitioning operational plans to continue providing food security to residents in Greene County in need, while simultaneously taking into consideration the safety of our staff, volunteers, partners and clients.

    In doing so, we are presuming an increased number of individuals in need food assistance, with more and more of the working class being affected by lay-offs.

    If you can contribute, please give. We appreciate your support!

    Contributing funds is the most efficient way to help. Additional funds help us purchase food at wholesale or less through the Feeding America network. Contributions help us keep our lights and refrigerators on, and ensure men, women and children facing hunger in Greene County have nourishing food during difficult times like these. For every $5 donated, we are able to purchase approximately $25 worth of food.

    Thank you for caring about hunger and for choosing to help, however you can. Together we can make a difference!



We have over 150 volunteers who work with our pantries to help pack and distribute monthly food boxes to about 2,000 individuals every month. We couldn’t do it without you!

About the Corner Cupboard Food Bank

The Corner Cupboard Food Bank is the only food bank in Greene County, responsible for providing regular monthly food boxes to individuals facing food insecurity in our county. We accomplish this by utilizing a network of pantries throughout the townships of Greene County. At the pantry level, individuals get to interact with their local pantry coordinator and volunteers to receive food assistance.
We rely on the generosity and support of individuals like you to continue to achieve our goals. Without community donations, serving our neighbors in need would not be possible.
